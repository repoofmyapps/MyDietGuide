What is the Egg Diet?

Egg diet is a diet made with boiled eggs.
1 Piece (Medium 50 gr) Boiled egg consists of approximately 78 kcal and contains approximately 6 g of protein. It is a very rich food in terms of vitamins and minerals. In addition, egg is a food that contains vitamins A, B2, B6, B12, Folic acid, D, E and minerals such as zinc, iron, calcium, phosphorus, copper, selenium.

Egg diet accelerates metabolism and helps to burn fat, so it helps the individual to lose weight in a short time.

Dietitians state that consuming 1 egg a day will not cause weight gain because of its high nutritional value. Although 1 egg is low in calories, it has an effect on the body when consumed too much. Those who are afraid of gaining weight can only consume the white part of the egg.
Do not neglect to drink water in the egg diet where you will consume plenty of boiled eggs. In addition to these, you should support it with simple exercises such as brisk walking, and avoid alcoholic, carbonated and sugary drinks.
